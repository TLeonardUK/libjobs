◆ priority

enum jobs::priority
strong

Priority of a job.

Defines how urgent a job is. The job scheduler will always attempt to execute higher priorities first.

Should be ordered most to least priority.

Enumerator
critical 

Critical priority jobs

high 

High priority jobs

normal 

Medium priority jobs

low 

Low priority jobs

slow 

Very slow and long running jobs should be assigned this priority, it allows easy segregation to prevent saturating thread pools.

count 
all 

All priorities together.

all_but_slow 

All priorities together except slow.
